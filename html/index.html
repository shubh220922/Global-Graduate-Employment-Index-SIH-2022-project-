GLOBAL GRADUATE EMPLOYMENT INDEX

The Global Graduate Employment Index is a research based record of the employability index of millions of graduating students around the world. The cumulative records and respective statistics based on the subjected industries are studied under the Global Graduate Employment Index. The aim of this project is to provide the respective institutes with relevant surveyed data that can enable them to successfully bifurcate employability chances of their graduates and to refurnish their course structures inorder to radically increase the chances of their graduates being hired. The provided data can also be beneficial to individuals proceeding to their graduation in their domain as it will help them understand the current day and future demand for thier skills.

The study under the Global Graduate Employment Index has been conducted under three specific sectors of academia, The field of engineering, pharmacy and management. Further clear bifurcations have been made in the statistical data provided based upon the selected industry of work. The study for individual sectors has accounted the data of the past years and also employement ratios of the respective industries under consideration.The data for sudden change of the employement landscape due to factors like economic recession and pandemic outbreak has not been provided before hand for drawing conclusions. Such factors are kept under research and the data regarding them is declared only when a substantial amount of evidence of their impact on the job market is procured.
