What we're about

Every year millions of graduates pass out from their respective institutions ranging form different fields of academia such as Engineering, Pharmacy and Management. Majority of such graduates move on to get employeed by different corporate companies. Such companies hire these graduates based on the skill-sets possessed by the graduates that they have acquired from the courses they have taken in college.

The Global Graduate Employability Index is a research based statistical and demograpghic study of the employment landscape of India and provides data related to graduate employability.

The study conducted under the Global Graduate Employability Index helps AICTE set general standard for various degree providing institutions to provide courses that educate the students about the in-demand skills required by the corporates.
